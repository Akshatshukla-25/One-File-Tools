Tips for effective alt text:
  • Describe the content and function, not just "image of…".
  • Keep it concise — aim for roughly 5–15 words (under ~125 characters).
  • Don't start with "picture of" or "image of" — screen readers already announce it's an image.
  • Convey context: why is this image here, what does it communicate?
  • Use empty alt="" for purely decorative images.
